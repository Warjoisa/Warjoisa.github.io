How To Play?

Use your arrows keys to move the DVD logo into a corner. You can make one move every 100 milliseconds. Each time the logo hits a corner you advance a level. The more levels you complete the faster the logo moves, and the harder it becomes to control, but the easier it becomes to score. What will your highscore be? This game doesn't save your highscore when you leave. Good luck! Fun challenge: Can you completely broke the game?
